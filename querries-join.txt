1. Selezionare tutti gli studenti iscritti al Corso di Laurea in Economia:
SELECT students.name, students.surname, students.registration_number, degrees.name AS 'corso_laurea'
FROM `students`
INNER JOIN degrees
ON students.degree_id = degrees.id
WHERE degrees.id = 53;
